Data Scientist/Machine Learning Scientist 

I am a data scientist by day and a machine learning engineer by night with over 5 years of experience in the Business Intelligence domain. With experience in multiple domains including E-commerce, Power, Manufacturing, IoT and Technology, I have several data analysis projects under my belt.


I am a big proponent of using data to justify all of our decisions and firmly believe in the power of deep learning and net neutrality. I am also passionate about learning new technologies & techniques (my latest obsession being Tensorflow). 


With two Master’s degree (in Business Administration and Chemistry) and a bachelors (in Electronics and Instrumentation), you can always find me engaging in productive discussions (or healthy debates). 


When I’m not learning the latest Data Science methodologies, you’ll probably find me playing Overwatch on my PS4 or reading Fantasy Fiction.
